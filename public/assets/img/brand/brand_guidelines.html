Hamburg Overhead Door Brand Guidelines 1

01 INTRODUCTION

Purpose & Intent

This brand guide ensures Hamburg Overhead Door  

is represented clearly and consistently across every  

touchpoint. It provides direction for how the brand  

should look and feel, helping all communications  

reinforce trust, professionalism, and quality.

A Shared Responsibility

As Hamburg Overhead Door continues to grow its  

marketing efforts, multiple people and partners  

contribute to how the brand is presented. This guide  

serves as a practical resource for employees, designers,  

vendors, and collaborators ensuring everyone  

communicates with consistency and confidence.

Consistency strengthens brand recognition and  

builds trust, reinforcing Hamburg Overhead Door  

as a dependable choice for homeowners.

The Ultimate Mission

This guide supports the Hamburg Overhead Door  

mission by ensuring every interaction—from signage  

and digital experiences to printed materials—feels  

intentional, professional, and aligned with the level  

of service customers expect.

02.3 LOGO USAGE

Logo usage

Correct  

logo use  

Please follow these guidelines  

to ensure brand consistency.

Logo safe area is determined by the space  

contained within the capital “H”.

Incorrect  

logo use  

Please follow these guidelines  

Use the full color logo over white, light  colors or lighter images with low contrast  to ensure optimum legibility.

Use the white logo over dark colors or  images with minimal visual distractions to  ensure optimum legibility.

to prevent inconsistent use  of the Hamburg Overhead  Door logo.

Do not change logo colors. Do not modify logo elements in any way. Do not distort logo.

Do not apply any visual treatments. Do not place logo over distracting backgrounds.

02.5 LAYOUT EXAMPLES

Image and color use

Images should complement  the layout—not compete  with it. When visuals and  content work together, the  message is clearer and  more impactful.

Full-bleed imagery (extending  beyond the page trim) can  be used to create a more  immersive, polished look.  This approach helps the  design feel more modern  and visually engaging.

Brand graphics

Use approved brand  

headers and/or footers in  conjunction with the logo.  Always follow established  

logo usage guidelines,  including proper sizing,  spacing, and safe area  requirements.
